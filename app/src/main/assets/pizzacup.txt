Pizza in a Cup Ingredients:
8 Tbsp all-purpose flour
¼ tsp baking powder
⅛ tsp baking soda 
6 Tbsp whole milk 
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil + more for greasing 
1 Tbsp pre-made pizza sauce 
2 Tbsp mozzarella, shredded
3 pepperoni slices, cut into small circles or quartered

1. Combine flour, baking powder, and baking soda in a small bowl and whisk to combine.

2. Add milk and olive oil to the bowl, and whisk to combine.

3. Transfer the dough mixture to a well-oiled oven-safe mug.

4. Bake at 375 °F for 15 minutes.

5. Remove the cup from the oven and top with pizza sauce and cheese.

6. Return the cup to the oven to allow the cheese to melt, about 3-4 minutes.

7. Top with pepperoni and return to the oven once more for an additional 4-5 minutes or until the top has crisped.
